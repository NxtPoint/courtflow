## 2. Content pillars (rotate these — never run out of ideas)
1. **Coaching value / tips** — short, useful tennis tips from Coach Ross & the team (serve, forehand, footwork). *Positions you as the experts.*
2. **Social proof** — testimonials, junior wins, member transformations, "6 months progress." *Builds trust.*
3. **The place & vibe** — the clay court, the 8 courts, sunrise hits, Killarney setting. *Your unfair advantage — show it off.*
4. **Programs & offers** — free first lesson, cardio tennis, junior squads, high-performance, Saturday social. *The conversion driver.*
5. **Community & behind-the-scenes** — coaches' personalities, club life, events, fun reels. *Makes you human + shareable.*

## 3. Formats & cadence (sustainable, not overwhelming)
- **Reels — 3×/week** (your #1 reach driver; short 7–20s clips, trending audio, captions on screen).
- **Carousels / single posts — 2×/week** (tips, testimonials, offers).
- **Stories — daily-ish** (low effort: courts today, a drill, a poll, repost a member tag). Stories keep you top-of-mind without polishing.
- Rule of thumb: **~80% value/vibe, ~20% direct selling.** Earn attention, then ask.
