Book a Tennis Court in Killarney, Johannesburg

18 July 2026

Looking for a tennis court near you in Johannesburg? At NextPoint Tennis in Killarney, you can book a court online in under a minute — no membership required — and play on any of our seven floodlit hard courts or the only clay court in Gauteng.

We're at Killarney Country Club in Houghton, a short drive from Rosebank, Parktown, Saxonwold and Sandton. So if you've been searching for tennis courts near you on the north side of Joburg, you've found your home court.

Book a court in 30 seconds

You don't have to be a member to play:

1. Open the booking page and choose Book a court. 2. Pick your surface — a hard court, or the clay court (listed separately). 3. Choose your time from the live availability and confirm. 4. Pay online, at the court, or on a monthly account.

Popular evening and weekend slots fill up fast — especially the clay court — so it's worth booking ahead.

Seven hard courts and the only clay court in Gauteng

Most clubs give you one surface. We give you a choice:

  • Seven resurfaced hard courts — fast, true, and floodlit for evening play.
  • Gauteng's only clay court — slower, higher bounce, kinder on the body, and the surface that builds complete players. Here's how clay changes your game.

Floodlit tennis, seven days a week

Every court is floodlit, so you can finish work, book a court, and play under lights. We're open seven days a week — early mornings, lunchtime hits, and evenings after the office.

What it costs

Courts are pay-as-you-go — book and play, no strings attached. If you play often, a membership works out better and includes a free trial week. You'll see the live price for your chosen time and surface right on the booking page before you pay.

New to NextPoint? Your first week is free — a perfect way to try the courts, a class, and the clay.

More than just courts

While you're here, NextPoint is also home to:

  • Private lessons with ATP-level coaches — your first lesson is free.
  • Group classes and cardio tennis — a fun, social way to improve and stay fit.
  • Junior and high-performance programmes for kids and competitive players.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be a member to book a court? No. Anyone can book and play pay-as-you-go. Membership simply saves you money if you play regularly.

Can I book a tennis court online? Yes — book hard or clay courts, lessons and classes in seconds at nextpointtennis.com.

Where are you located? Killarney Country Club, Houghton, Johannesburg — close to Rosebank, Parktown and Sandton.

Are there public tennis courts near me in Johannesburg? NextPoint's courts are open to the public to book — you don't need to be a club member. If you're in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg, we're one of the easiest courts to reserve online.

Do you have floodlights for evening tennis? Yes — every court is floodlit, seven days a week.
