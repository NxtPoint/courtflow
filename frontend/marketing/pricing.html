Good to know

Pricing, answered.

Should I pay as I go or become a member?
Pay as you go if you play now and then — courts from R90 and lessons from R250, no commitment. If you book courts more than a couple of times a month, membership pays for itself: members book courts free and get member pricing across the club.
What does the free week include?
7 days of full access — book courts, try a class and see live coach availability. No commitment, and no pressure to continue.
Do members pay for lessons?
Yes — membership covers court bookings only. Lessons and classes are booked and paid separately, with member pricing.
Can I pay online, or at the club?
Both. Pay securely online — card, Apple Pay, Google Pay or Samsung Pay — or settle at the desk when you play. Memberships and packs can be bought online or at the club too.
Can I book and pay for my family?
Yes — add your children as dependents and book courts, lessons and classes for them from your own login, with everything on one account.
