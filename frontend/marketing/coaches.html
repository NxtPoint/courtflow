The founders

Two careers at the top of the game.

Program Director

Neville Godwin

2017 ATP Coach of the Year

Coaching career highlights

  • Coached Kevin Anderson to the world top 10 and the 2017 US Open final — the form that earned Neville the 2017 ATP Coach of the Year award.
  • Guided Hyeon Chung to the 2018 Australian Open semi-finals — the first South Korean player to reach a Grand Slam semi.
  • Worked with Reilly Opelka on tour.
  • With Xavier Malisse, helped develop Alexei Popyrin — including his first ATP Masters 1000 title (Montreal, 2024).

Coaching philosophy

A holistic approach — technical skill, tactical awareness, physical conditioning and mental toughness — with training tailored to each player's individual needs, on and off the court.

Head Coach

Ross Nemeth

30+ years developing champions

Coaching career highlights

  • Coached numerous national junior champions in singles and doubles, and top ITF junior players.
  • Guided a player from age 13 to the U18 National Championship and the Junior French Open — culminating in a full scholarship at a top-10 NCAA Division 1 college in the USA.
  • International experience: the Dutch National training facility and ITF events including the Junior Fed Cup (Barcelona), the Astrid Bowl (Belgium) and the Junior French Open.

Coaching philosophy

Develops well-rounded players — stroke mechanics and footwork, game strategy and match analysis, strength and agility, and mental toughness — to prepare athletes for success on and off the court.
